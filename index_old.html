Before you start
The order is fixed from hole 1. The player first in order has the Honor on hole 1, then it rotates.

The Honor tees off first. Most points after 18 holes wins. On a tied hole, no points are awarded.

4 players — holes 17 & 18: The player with fewest points has the Honor.
How the Honor chooses
🔴
Blind Wolf
Declared before own tee shot. Goes solo vs all.
🟡
Lone Wolf
Declared after own tee shot, before others hit. Goes solo vs all.
⚪
Wolf
Honor has not chosen a partner after all hit — automatically solo.
🟢
Partner
Honor picks a partner right after they hit. The unpicked player becomes the lone Wolf.
