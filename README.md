# 🐺 Wolf Score

A free, mobile-friendly scoring app for the Wolf golf game. Works on both iPhone and Android — install it directly from your browser as an app, no App Store required.

**[▶ Play now](https://birre99-art.github.io/Wolf-score/)**

---

## What is Wolf?

Wolf is one of golf's most strategic and exciting betting games. Players rotate as the "Wolf" on each hole, deciding whether to take on everyone solo or pick a partner. High risk, high reward — every hole is different.

## Features

- ✅ Supports **3 and 4 players**
- ✅ **Configurable number of rounds** (1–20) — perfect for multi-day trips
- ✅ Correct scoring for all Wolf variants: Blind Wolf, Lone Wolf, Wolf and Partner play
- ✅ **Special rule for holes 17 & 18** (4-player): player with fewest points gets Wolf honors
- ✅ Scores are **automatically saved** between sessions — safe to close the app
- ✅ Protection against accidentally starting a new tournament and losing data
- ✅ **Share results** via WhatsApp or as a graphic image
- ✅ Built-in **rules page** for quick reference on the course
- ✅ Works as a **PWA** — install on home screen like a native app

## Scoring

### 3 players
| Outcome | Points |
|---|---|
| Blind Wolf wins | +4p |
| Lone Wolf wins | +3p |
| Wolf wins | +2p |
| Opponents win (vs solo) | +1p each |
| Partner play — winning side | +1p each |
| Tied hole | 0p |

### 4 players
| Outcome | Points |
|---|---|
| Blind Wolf wins | +5p |
| Lone Wolf wins | +4p |
| Wolf wins | +3p |
| Opponents win (vs solo) | +1p each |
| Partner play 2v2 — winning side | +1p each |
| Tied hole | 0p |

## How to play

1. Draw a random order before the first hole — this order is fixed for the entire round
2. The **first player in the order is the Wolf** on hole 1, then it rotates
3. The Wolf tees off first, then decides:
   - **Blind Wolf** — declare solo *before* teeing off (highest reward)
   - **Lone Wolf** — declare solo *after own tee shot*, before others hit
   - **Wolf** — go solo after seeing all tee shots (or automatically if no partner chosen)
   - **Partner** — choose a partner *immediately* after they hit — you cannot wait to see other players hit first!
4. Lowest score wins the hole for their side
5. Tied holes — no points awarded
6. **4-player special rule:** The player with the fewest points gets Wolf honors on holes 17 and 18
