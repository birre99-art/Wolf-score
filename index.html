Before you start
The order is drawn randomly before the first hole and is fixed for the entire round.

The player who is first in the order has the Honor on hole 1, then it rotates hole by hole.

The Honor tees off first on every hole. Most points after 18 holes wins. On a tied hole, no points are awarded.

4 players — holes 17 & 18: The player with fewest points has the Honor on hole 17. The player with fewest points after hole 17 has the Honor on hole 18.
How the Honor chooses
