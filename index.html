Before you start
The order is the one you enter before hole 1 and is fixed for the entire round.

The player who is first in the order has the Honor on hole 1, then it rotates hole by hole.

The Honor tees off first on every hole. Most points after all holes wins. On a tied hole, no points are awarded.

4 players — last 2 holes: The player with fewest points has the Honor. Ties are broken by rotation order.
How the Honor chooses
🔴
Blind Wolf
Declared before the Honor tees off. Goes solo vs all.
🟡
Lone Wolf
Declared after own tee shot, but before others hit. Goes solo vs all.
⚪
Honor
Has not chosen before all others hit — automatically goes solo.
🟢
Partner
Choose a partner immediately after they hit. The remaining player automatically becomes the Wolf.
