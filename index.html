Before you start
The order is the one you enter before hole 1 and is fixed for the entire round.

The player who is first in the order has the Honor on hole 1, then it rotates hole by hole.

The Honor tees off first on every hole. Most points after 18 holes wins. On a tied hole, no points are awarded.

4 players — holes 17 & 18: The player with fewest points has the Honor on hole 17. The player with fewest points after hole 17 has the Honor on hole 18.
How the Honor chooses
🔴
Blind Wolf
Declared before the Honor tees off. Goes solo vs all.
🟡
Lone Wolf
Declared after own tee shot, but before others hit. Goes solo vs all.
⚪
Honor
The Honor has not chosen a partner before all others hit — automatically goes solo.
🟢
Partner
The Honor chooses a partner immediately after they hit. You cannot wait to see others hit first. The player left alone automatically becomes the Wolf.
